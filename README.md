Netflix Data Analysis

Overview:
This project analyzes the Netflix dataset to understand trends in content growth, distribution, and user preferences across different content types and countries.



Objectives:
Analyze growth of Netflix content over time  
Compare Movies and TV Shows trends  
Study country-wise contribution (India vs United States)  
Understand content ratings, duration, and viewer preferences  
Identify patterns in content addition and release  



Tools and Technologies:
Python  
Pandas  
Matplotlib  



Dataset:
The dataset contains information about Netflix movies and TV shows, including title, type, country, release year, rating, duration, and date added.



Key Insights:

1.Content Distribution and Growth
- Movies dominate Netflix content with 6131 titles compared to 2666 TV Shows  
- Peak content addition occurred in 2019  
- Content addition increased rapidly after 2015, followed by slower growth  
- There is generally a 1-year gap between content release and its addition to Netflix  


2.Movies vs TV Shows Trend
- Movies were significantly more common initially  
- TV Shows have increased over time  
- Suggests a gradual shift in viewer preference toward series-based content  



3.Country-wise Analysis (India vs United States)
- United States leads in total content production  
- India has shown strong growth over time  
- In 2018, India nearly matched US content levels  
- India is emerging as a major contributor to Netflix content  



4.Ratings Analysis
- Most content falls under TV-MA and TV-14 ratings  
- Indicates that the majority of content targets adults and teenagers  
- This trend is consistent across both Movies and TV Shows  



5.Duration and Viewer Preferences

TV Shows
- Majority of TV Shows have only 1 season (1793 shows)  
- 2-season shows are significantly fewer (421 shows)  
- Number of shows decreases as seasons increase  

Movies
- Most movies are around 90 minutes in duration  
- Indicates preference for shorter content formats  



6.Directors Analysis
- Top contributing directors include:
- Rajiv Chilaka (19 titles)  
- Raúl Campos & Jan Suter (18 titles)  
- Suhas Kadav and Marcus Raboy (16 titles)  
- A small group of directors contributes multiple titles  



7.Monthly Trends
- Content is added fairly consistently throughout the year  
- Highest additions occur in:
  July (827 titles)  
  December (813 titles)  



8.Genre Analysis
- International Movies dominate the platform  
- Dramas and Comedies are also highly prevalent  
- Indicates strong global content consumption  



Conclusion
Netflix has expanded rapidly since 2015, reaching peak content addition in 2019. While movies still dominate the platform, there is a noticeable shift toward TV Shows. The platform is increasingly investing in international content, with countries like India emerging as major contributors. Viewer preferences indicate a strong inclination toward shorter and engaging content formats, primarily targeting adults and teenagers.
